📖 How to Simulate

1

Group Stage

Click on 3 teams from each group to select: 1st place (gold), 2nd place (silver), and 3rd place (bronze). You must select at least 1st and 2nd from all 12 groups to advance.

2

Best 3rd Place

After groups, select exactly 8 of the 12 third-place teams. These 8 teams will fill the remaining 8 spots to make 32 teams total for Round of 32.

3

Knockout Rounds

Round of 32 → Round of 16 → Quarterfinals → Semifinals → Final. For each match, click on the team you think will win. Winners advance to the next round.

4

Champion!

After the Final, see your simulated World Cup champion! You can share the result or start a new simulation.

💡 Tips

  • Click team flags and names to select them
  • You can go back to previous stages using the navigation tabs
  • Use the Reset button to start over anytime
  • Change language anytime with the language selector
